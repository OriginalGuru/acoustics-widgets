Vibration recipe
Strike r/R0.500
Amplitude = |Jm(βmn r/R)| — the total energy deposited in mode (m,n) is Jm²(β·r/R)·(cos²(mθ)+sin²(mθ)) = Jm²(β·r/R). The angle-independent Pythagorean identity means dragging azimuthally rotates the pattern without changing the recipe. Moving r to a nodal ring of Jm zeros that mode entirely.

Decay τn = τ₁ / ration — radiation-dominated damping scales as f (not f²), so overtones decay more gradually relative to the fundamental than in a bar. The inharmonic spectrum combined with this milder roll-off gives drums their characteristic pitch ambiguity.

Beating — for m≥1 the two degenerate partners (cos and sin eigenfunctions) have slightly different frequencies on any real drum. A random split δf ~ 0.5–2.5 Hz is applied in synthesis, producing amplitude beating whose rate depends on drum imperfection.
